nova

Privacy Policy — Last updated: 2026-05-08 — App version: 28.4.0

Summary

Nova is a personal AI voice assistant. The Nova app on your iPhone connects to a Nova server operated by FxLooper (“we”, “us”). Your conversations are processed through our server using the Anthropic Claude AI API. We take your privacy seriously and comply with GDPR and applicable data protection laws.

Data Controller

FxLooper (Ondřej Hladík)
Email: fxlooper.business@gmail.com
Based in: Czech Republic, EU

What data we collect and why

Account data

  • What: Name, email, city, language preference, timezone, subscription plan.
  • Why: To personalize Nova’s responses and manage your subscription.
  • Legal basis: Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

Conversation data

  • What: Text messages you send and Nova’s responses.
  • Why: To provide AI assistant functionality and maintain conversation context.
  • Retention: Stored on our server for 90 days, then automatically deleted. You can delete anytime.
  • Legal basis: Contract performance.

Audio (microphone)

  • What: Voice input for speech-to-text.
  • Processing: Speech recognition runs on your iPhone using WhisperKit (on-device). Raw audio is never sent to our server or any third party. Only the transcribed text is sent.
  • Legal basis: Consent (microphone permission).

Voice ID (optional)

  • What: Voice profile for speaker verification.
  • Processing: Stored on our server, used only to verify your identity.
  • Legal basis: Consent (you choose to enroll).

Photos and videos (optional)

  • What: Images you send for Nova to analyze.
  • Processing: Sent to our server → Anthropic Claude API for analysis → response returned → image deleted from server within 24 hours.
  • Legal basis: Consent (you choose to send).

Location (optional)

  • What: Approximate location (city-level).
  • Why: Weather, nearby places, local information.
  • Processing: Used only for the specific query, not stored permanently.
  • Legal basis: Consent (location permission).

Usage data

  • What: Token consumption per model (Opus/Sonnet/Haiku), number of API calls.
  • Why: To enforce subscription limits and calculate costs.
  • What we do NOT track: specific content of your messages, browsing history, app usage patterns.
  • Legal basis: Legitimate interest (billing).

Push notification tokens

  • What: Apple Push Notification device token.
  • Why: To send you scheduled tasks, briefings, and proactive notifications.
  • Legal basis: Consent (notification permission).

Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  1. Access — See all data we hold about you. Request via email or in-app.
  2. Rectification — Correct inaccurate data. Change your profile in Settings.
  3. Erasure (“Right to be forgotten”) — Delete all your data. In-app: Settings → Delete History. Full account deletion: email us.
  4. Data portability — Export your data in machine-readable format. In-app: Settings → Export Conversation.
  5. Restriction — Limit how we process your data. Email us.
  6. Object — Object to processing based on legitimate interest. Email us.
  7. Withdraw consent — Revoke permissions anytime in iOS Settings → Nova.

We respond to all GDPR requests within 30 days.

Data deletion

In-app deletion

  • Conversation history: Settings → Delete History (immediate, irreversible).
  • Voice ID profile: Settings → Voice ID → Delete Profile.
  • Push tokens: Uninstall the app.

Full account deletion

Email fxlooper.business@gmail.com with subject “Delete my Nova account”. We will delete all your data within 30 days and confirm by email.

Automatic deletion

  • Conversation history: auto-deleted after 90 days.
  • Images/videos: deleted within 24 hours of processing.
  • Usage logs: retained for 12 months for billing, then deleted.

Data export

In-app: Settings → Export Conversation — exports your full chat history as a text file. For a complete data export (including server-side data): email us.

Data security

  • All connections use TLS/HTTPS encryption in transit.
  • Server credentials stored in iOS Keychain (hardware-encrypted).
  • Server access restricted by unique authentication tokens per client.
  • Client data isolated — each client can only access their own data.
  • No shared database — each client has separate storage.

Children

Nova is rated 4+ but subscription services are intended for users 16+. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

International transfers

Your data is processed on servers located in the European Union (Czech Republic). If Anthropic processes data outside the EU, they do so under Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as per their privacy policy.

Data breach notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will notify you via email and/or push notification within 72 hours as required by GDPR Article 33.

Changes to this policy

We will notify you of material changes via:

  • Push notification in the app
  • Updated “Last updated” date
  • Release notes in App Store

Supervisory authority

If you are not satisfied with how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In Czech Republic: ÚOOÚ (Úřad pro ochranu osobních údajů), www.uoou.cz.
